How to Make Rice

Stove-top Rice Guide

Four easy steps to make delicious rice.

  1. You need about 1 to 1.5 ratio of rice and water
  2. Rinse the rice about 2 times to get clean rice
  3. Now you should boil the rice for about 18 minutes
  4. After 18 minutes, let the rice rest for some time and now you got good rice to eat.

That is the best way to make rice.
